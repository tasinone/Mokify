About Mokify

Why Mokify Exists

Finding a simple, free tool to create professional mockups from screenshots shouldn't be difficult. Most existing solutions lack essential features like mobile mockups, shadow options, or require expensive subscriptions.

Mokify was born from this frustration. We wanted to create something elegant and easy-to-use for showcasing website and mobile app screenshots.

Whether you're a designer presenting to clients, a developer showcasing your work, or a startup building your portfolio, Mokify makes it effortless to create stunning presentation-ready mockups.

Powerful Features

Everything you need to create professional mockups that impress clients and showcase your work beautifully.

Multi-Device Support

Create mockups for desktop, mobile, or both side-by-side. Perfect for showcasing responsive designs and cross-platform applications.

Theme Customization

Switch between light and dark themes to match your design aesthetic. Frames automatically adapt to complement your screenshots.

Professional Shadows

Add depth and realism with customizable shadow effects. Choose from multiple shadow styles to make your mockups stand out.

Background Control

Customize background colors and add rounded edges. Create the perfect canvas that complements your design presentation.

Flexible Padding

Adjust vertical and horizontal padding to create the perfect composition. Fine-tune spacing for optimal visual balance.

High-Quality Export

Download your mockups in original resolution with perfect quality. Ready for presentations, portfolios, and client deliverables.
